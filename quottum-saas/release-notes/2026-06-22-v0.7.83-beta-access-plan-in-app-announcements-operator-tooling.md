## Beta program
- **In-app announcements** — beta testers now see a dismissible "New beta feature available" banner in the dashboard when they're granted one, on top of the email.
- **Beta access comp plan** — a new free, private plan that unlocks the whole product (every feature, no limits); assign it to a tester's workspace in one step.
- Feedback **screenshots show inline** in the operator feedback view instead of a text link.

## Operator console
- **Impersonate any workspace** — operators can now view and act in any workspace for support without being a member of it.
- **Operators separated from customers** — the Users list badges operators and defaults to a Customers view, and customer metrics (MAU, totals, signups) no longer count internal accounts.
