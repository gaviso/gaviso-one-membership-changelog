Public pricing page is live; signup now carries the chosen plan through to the workspace; Stripe-backed credit balance + in-app cancellation policy that issues credit instead of refunds.

## 💰 Pricing live

- Public pricing is live at **[quottum.io/pricing](https://quottum.io/pricing)** — pick **Free**, **Pro**, or **Business** and your plan choice carries through signup.
- **14-day free trial on Pro** — no credit card required up front.

## 💳 Account credit replaces refunds

- Cancelling a paid plan mid-cycle now **issues account credit** for unused time, applied automatically to your next invoice.
- No more partial refunds — the credit lives on your Stripe customer and is consumed by the next charge.

## 🧾 New billing dashboard

At `/dashboard/settings/billing`:

- **Credit balance** (with cycle-end date if applicable).
- **Current plan + cycle** (monthly / yearly / semi-annual).
- **Trial countdown** if you're on a Pro trial.
- **In-app payment-method update** (Stripe Elements — card details never touch Quottum).
- **In-app cancel** — confirms the credit you'll receive before you commit.

## 🛠 Operator controls

In `/admin/workspaces`:

- **Per-plan trial length** — set or change trial days per plan.
- **Issue credit** modal — manual credit adjustments to any workspace, with audit trail.
- **Trial-eligibility reset** — for support cases where a workspace should be allowed a fresh trial.
