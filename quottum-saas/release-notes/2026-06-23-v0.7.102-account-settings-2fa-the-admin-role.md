## What's new
- **Two-factor authentication (2FA)** — secure your account with an authenticator app from the new **My Account** settings. Once enabled, you'll enter a 6-digit code at sign-in.
- **Personal account settings** — upload an avatar, and change your name, email, and password in one place. The email field now shows whether it's verified or a change is pending.
- **Users & Permissions** — the renamed team page lists everyone in your workspace by role with inline role changes, plus a new **Admin** role: near-owner access for everything except transferring ownership or deleting the workspace.
