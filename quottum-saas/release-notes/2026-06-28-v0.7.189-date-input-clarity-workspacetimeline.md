### Fixed

- **Change subscription modal date inputs show the actual stored value above the input** ("Currently: <date>" or "Currently: not set"). Empty \`<input type="date">\` was rendering today's date as the browser's locale placeholder, making a null \`current_period_end\` look like a committed signup-date value. The new line reads the prop directly, independent of the browser's painting.

### Added

- **\`<WorkspaceTimeline>\` card on \`/admin/workspaces/[id]\`** — compact vertical timeline of lifecycle dates pulled from the workspace row only (no audit join). Renders Workspace created, Trial started, Trial ends, and Current period ends / Access ends with absolute dates + relative hints ("14 days ago", "in 14 days", "today"). Past events are dimmed, today's accent-colored, future ones regular ink. When no event lands on today, a soft "Today" reference is inserted between past and future so the cursor of "where are we now" is always visible. Missing fields are skipped — a fresh Free signup just shows "Workspace created".
