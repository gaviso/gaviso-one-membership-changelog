### Added
- 📎 **File attachments** — attach files to any quote or contract by drag & drop (up to 10 MB each)
- Preview images and PDFs inline, or download them with one click
- Files appear on the client-facing quote and contract pages, and on PDF documents
- Per-file **Client-visible** toggle to control exactly what each client can see
