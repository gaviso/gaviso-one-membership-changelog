## What's new
- **Per-plan attachment storage caps** — Free 100 MB · Pro 1 GB · Business 10 GB. Settings → Data shows your usage with a progress bar that warns at 80% and blocks new uploads when you're at the limit (existing files keep working).

## Operator console
- **One-click beta review** — approving a tester now also assigns the **Beta access** comp plan automatically; rejecting activates the workspace on the Free plan. No separate "now go change the plan" step.
- **Editable storage caps per plan** on the plans matrix.
- **Storage & data transfer on Metrics** — a new section with a Today / This week / This month / This quarter / This year filter. Storage shows current total across all workspaces; data transfer shows bytes uploaded + downloaded in the chosen period.
