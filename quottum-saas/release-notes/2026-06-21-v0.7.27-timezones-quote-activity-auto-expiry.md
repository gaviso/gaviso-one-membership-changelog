## 🌍 Per-workspace timezone

Set your timezone in **Settings → General**. Every date and time across Quottum — your dashboard, quotes, contracts, clients, activity history, the pages your clients see, and PDFs — now displays in your workspace timezone. Quote expiry is calculated in your timezone too.

## 📜 Quote activity log

Each quote now has a full, detailed activity timeline: created, sent, viewed by the client, accepted or declined, revision requested, plus granular edits (line items, pricing, discounts, valid-until date, billing period), file changes, and conversion to a contract. Long histories paginate.

## ⏰ Automatic quote expiry

Quotes past their "valid until" date now move to **Expired** on their own — no manual cleanup needed.

## 📎 File sharing controls

Choose whether each attached file is visible to your client. Client-visible files now appear on the client-facing quote and contract pages, and are listed with download links on the PDF.

## ✨ Client-facing polish

- Editable company **slogan / tagline** (Branding) shown on the client quote view and on PDFs
- Client view now shows **"Awaiting your approval"** with clickable contact details and the valid-until date
- **Client View** button on accepted quotes
- **"Powered by Quottum"** on PDF documents
- Public quote and contract pages always render in light mode
