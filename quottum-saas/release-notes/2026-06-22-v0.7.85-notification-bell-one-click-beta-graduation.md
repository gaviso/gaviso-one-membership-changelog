## What's new
- **Notification bell** — a "What's new" bell in the top bar lists recent product updates (pulled from the changelog), with an unread dot so you never miss a release.

## Behind the scenes (operator / beta program)
- **Self-service graduate** — promote a beta feature to a paid plan feature (pick the plans) or to free-for-everyone, in one click on the Beta testers page. No code change needed.
- **Beta badge** — a reusable "Beta" marker for in-progress features that automatically disappears once a feature graduates.
