Follow-up to v0.7.211's duplicate-invoice fix. v0.7.211 stopped NEW orphaned invoices from being created (cancel stale incomplete subs before creating one); this hides the ones already left behind.

The `/api/billing/invoices` route now shows every **paid** invoice (full history) plus anything tied to the workspace's **current active subscription** — so a genuinely-owed open invoice on the live sub still appears — and drops the rest. This hides the `open` invoices that abandoned/failed `default_incomplete` subscribe attempts leave behind (they never get paid and Stripe auto-voids them ~24h later).

Basil note: the invoice→subscription link now lives on `invoice.parent.subscription_details.subscription`.
