The 'Review change' step now shows the proration breakdown behind the net — the credit for unused time on the current plan + the charge for the new plan's remaining period (Stripe's own line descriptions), credits in green with a minus, charges plain — for both the charge and the credit (downgrade) cases. The preview route surfaces Stripe's createPreview `lines.data`; the modal renders them above the 'Due today' / 'Credited' total.
