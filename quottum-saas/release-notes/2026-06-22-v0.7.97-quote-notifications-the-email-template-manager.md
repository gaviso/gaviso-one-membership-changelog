## What's new
- **You're notified on every quote decision.** When a client accepts, declines, or requests changes on a quote, the workspace owner now gets an email — including the client's note and a direct link to the quote. Revision requests, which previously sent nothing, now also send the client a confirmation.

## Improvements
- **Beta: Client CSV export** — export your client list to CSV (rolling out to beta testers first).

## Behind the scenes (operator console)
- **Email template manager** — one branded master design wraps every message; edit each email type's subject + content with a live preview, a real code editor, and a one-click "Send test".
- Email **provider & sender** moved into a Resend card on the Integrations page; email **send history** folded into a filterable Audit log.
