## Privacy
- **Support access is now opt-in.** Quottum support can no longer access your workspace unless an **owner or admin** explicitly enables **Support access** in **Settings → General**. It's **off by default**, and any access is logged. Turn it on only while you need hands-on help, then switch it back off.

## Operator console (internal)
- Clearer revenue labels ("Contract MRR" — your customers' contract value, distinct from subscription revenue) and a distinct Overview icon.
