## 📤 Export your data

A new **Settings → Data & tables** lets you download your clients, quotes, and contracts as **CSV** (for spreadsheets & accounting) or **JSON** (full-fidelity, with line items). There's also a **full backup** — either data-only, or a complete **ZIP that bundles every uploaded file** with a manifest. Owner-only.

## 📥 Import clients from a CSV

Bring your client list in from a spreadsheet: upload a CSV, map the columns (auto-detected from your headers), preview a per-row plan with validation, then import. Duplicate clients (matched by email) are skipped automatically — so it's safe to re-run.

## 📨 Referral invites

The **Refer** page can now invite people to Quottum **by email**, not just by sharing a link. Track everyone you've invited and **resend** or **revoke** any invite.

## ✨ Polish

- **Colored client avatars** — clients without a logo now get a distinct, colorful initials badge (workspaces too).
- **Page loading states** — a smooth skeleton appears while a page loads.
- The browser-tab **favicon** is now the Quottum mark.
