## Operator console — Beta tester comp
Beta testers now retain **"Powered by Quottum"** on their public quotes and contracts even though their workspace is on the all-features comp plan. Other Business-exclusive features (Client CSV export, Multiple workspaces) are still comped — only the white-label carve-out is denied, so prospects who see a beta tester's document still see the brand.

Under the hood: new `plans.excluded_features` column. If a plan has `grants_all_features = true`, the comp set is now `all_active_features − excluded_features` (seeded with `white_label` on `beta_access`). Future carve-outs are a one-row update, no code change needed.
