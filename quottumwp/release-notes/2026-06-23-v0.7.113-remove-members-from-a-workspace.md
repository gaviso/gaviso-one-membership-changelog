## What's new
- **Remove a teammate from your workspace.** Owners and admins can now remove a member directly from **Settings → Users and Permissions** — previously you could only change someone's role. The last owner is protected (you can't accidentally orphan the workspace), and a removed member's account and data are untouched, so you can re-invite them later.
