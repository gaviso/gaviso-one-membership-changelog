## What's new
- **Send to client, properly.** Sending a quote now opens a real composer — pick your recipient(s), CC anyone, edit the subject, and tweak the message in a rich-text editor before it goes out. Re-send a quote any time; if your client just requested a revision, the email automatically uses the revised template.
- **Automatic quote reminders.** Nudge clients on a cadence you set (default 3 and 7 days after sending). Configure in **Settings → Reminder windows**.
- **Quote sends on the Activity timeline.** Every email a quote triggers shows up on its Activity panel — at a glance you can see who got what and when.
- **Unpublish a quote.** Realized something needs fixing right after publishing? One-click revert to draft.

## Beta program
- **Public beta signup at `/beta-signup`** — anyone can apply with a short intake (company, primary use, what they currently use, how they heard about us). Applications land on a waitlist for operator review; once approved, the workspace activates and the welcome email goes out.
- **Welcome card on the dashboard** for new testers — a 3-step checklist (add a client → create a quote → tour Settings) that ticks itself as you go.
- Beta testers are now one-workspace-per-person (existing testers grandfathered).

## Behind the scenes
- Quottum platform operators are kept out of any workspace and can't sign up as beta testers (staff vs. customers stays clean).
